Recycling

We used recycling of paper and metal, glass, plastic information for the years 2005-2019, as this was the most complete set of information for all five NYC boroughs and was comprised of 10,620 observations for paper recycling and 10,620 observations for metal, glass, and plastic recycling. The proportion of waste comprised of refuse, paper, and metal, glass, plastic respectively changed only slightly over the decade for which data were available. For further details, visit github.
